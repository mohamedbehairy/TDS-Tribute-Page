The Legend, The Icon

About Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward. He is regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time, having achieved numerous awards and accolades throughout his career. Known for his exceptional skills, dedication, and work ethic, Ronaldo has inspired millions worldwide.

Achievements

  • Ballon d'Or Wins

    2008, 2013, 2014, 2016, 2017

    Ronaldo has won the Ballon d'Or award five times, making him one of the most decorated footballers in the world.

  • European Golden Shoe Wins

    2008, 2014, 2015, 2016

    He has been the top scorer in European leagues on multiple occasions, reflecting his incredible goal-scoring ability.

  • UEFA Champions League Titles

    2008, 2014, 2016, 2017

    Ronaldo has won the UEFA Champions League four times, showcasing his excellence in Europe’s premier club competition.

  • Top Scorer in UEFA Champions League

    Most goals scored in the competition's history

    He holds the record for the most goals scored in the UEFA Champions League, demonstrating his consistent high performance.

  • Portugal’s Euro 2016 Victory

    Led Portugal to win the UEFA European Championship

    Ronaldo played a crucial role in leading Portugal to victory in the Euro 2016, adding an international trophy to his collection.
